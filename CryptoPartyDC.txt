    What are we doing?

	Discussing why cryptography, anonymity and anti-tracking tools are
	    important today.
	Installing online anonymity tools and learning to use them safely.
	Securing communications using SSH, SSL, VPNs, OTR, encrypted telephony
	    and instant messaging software.
	Encrypting e-mail with PGP and GnuPG and integrating them into your
	    e-mail software.
	Protecting information on our hard drives and USB keys from theft with
	    disk encryption.
	Making sure we can't be easily tracked online using tools like Tor.
	Installing and using network forensics tools to find out whether we've
	    done a good job!

	Pizza will be eaten.  Drinks will be had (BYO).
